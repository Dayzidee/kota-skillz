# 5 Creative Button Styles

Buttons are key interaction points. Here are five creative ways to design buttons on a website to improve user engagement:

## 1. Graphic Hover Button
*   **Interaction:** On hover, a distinct shape appears inside the button bounds.
*   **Elements:** Can utilize a star, a blob, or any SVG form. 
*   **Effect:** Makes the entire Call-to-Action (CTA) feel alive and playful.

## 2. Press Button
*   **Default State:** Sits with a visible outline and a strong drop shadow on the bottom, giving it a 3D, raised appearance.
*   **Interaction:** On hover or click, the button pushes down and the shadow disappears. 
*   **Effect:** Feels like you are physically pressing a mechanical button.

## 3. Skeuomorphic Button
*   **Visuals:** Features a raised, three-dimensional shape with a shadow carved around the inside edge.
*   **Effect:** Looks mimicking an actual, physical keyboard button placed directly on the screen.

## 4. Glassy Button
*   **Visuals:** Has a soft, translucent surface with a subtle light reflection along the top edge.
*   **Effect:** Gives the illusion that the button is crafted out of frosted glass.

## 5. Underline Button
*   **Minimalism:** Strips away all borders, backgrounds, and shadows.
*   **Visuals:** Leaves only the raw text and a single, crisp underline beneath it.
